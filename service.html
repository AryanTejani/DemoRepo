About Our History

Education is about learning skills and knowledge

Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT) is named to commemorate the region of Charotar - the golden land. CHARUSAT was accorded the status of a university under Gujarat State Act No. 8 of 2009 and is empowered to award degrees by UGC under Section 22 of the UGC Act, 1956.

  • Recognized for its contributions to higher education.
  • Committed to fostering research and development.
  • State-of-the-art facilities and resources.
  • Dedicated to holistic student development.
