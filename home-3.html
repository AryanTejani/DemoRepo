Assignment Submission

Our portal allows students to submit their assignments effortlessly in various formats. With automated deadline tracking and timely reminders, students can stay organized and ensure on-time submissions.

  • Easy upload process.
  • Multiple file format support.
  • Automated deadline tracking.
  • Timely reminders and notifications.

Grading and Feedback

Educators can efficiently grade assignments online, providing detailed feedback and grades that students can access instantly. This helps students understand their performance and areas for improvement.

  • Efficient online grading.
  • Instant feedback for students.
  • Detailed comments and suggestions.
  • Accessible grade records.

Progress Tracking

Both students and educators can track academic progress over time with detailed reports and analytics. Our dashboard provides a clear snapshot of current assignments, grades, and upcoming deadlines.

  • Detailed progress reports.
  • Analytics for performance tracking.
  • User-friendly dashboard.
  • Snapshot of grades and deadlines.

Academic Calendar

Stay updated with important academic dates and events through our integrated calendar. It includes assignment deadlines, exam schedules, and other key academic milestones.

  • Integrated academic calendar.

  • Customizable notifications.
